Title: Machine Learning vs. Deep Learning

Purpose: To compare machine learning and deep learning through practical business examples and explain when each approach should be used.

Audience: Technology professionals, recruiters, students, business leaders, and anyone interested in understanding artificial intelligence concepts.

This artifact was created as part of my graduate coursework in Machine Learning Fundamentals. The report compares machine learning and deep learning using Gmail spam detection and image recognition as practical examples. It demonstrates my ability to analyze AI concepts, evaluate different approaches, and communicate technical information to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Use of Generative AI

This artifact was developed with assistance from ChatGPT as a learning and writing support tool. I used generative AI to improve the organization, clarity, grammar, and presentation of the report while ensuring that I understood the concepts, verified the information, and made the final decisions regarding the content included in this artifact.

Intended Audience

This artifact is intended for students, technology professionals, recruiters, and business leaders who want to better understand the differences between machine learning and deep learning and how each technology can be applied to solve different business problems.
